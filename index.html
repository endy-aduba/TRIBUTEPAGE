A Tribute To
Barbara Aduba

Gospel Superstar

Barbara Aduba is a popular gospel artiste in Nigeria. How did Barbara Aduba become one of the most appreciated gospel singers in her country? What is her secret? This article provides a short tale about Barbara Aduba. It highlights how a regular girl became a superstar!

Barbara was born in Edo state, Nigeria on June 15th, 1993. She comes from a humble family, having just one sibling. This talented young lady became a blessing for the Christian World at the very young age. She started practicing music at the tender age of 10. Her vocal prowess opened doors for her to play on the largest stages in the country. Barbara is a member of Triumphant Family Church. The Church played a crucial role in building her career. She also refers to Pastor Tony Aduba who so happens to be not only her father, but also her Pastor and the General Overseer of Triumphant Family Church as her teacher and mentor. He was the man who spurred her into becoming the superstar that she is today.
